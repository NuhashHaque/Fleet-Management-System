# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Provide feedback focused on the work, not the person.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language
- Personal attacks or threats
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other
contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report behavior that violates this policy, open a private security advisory
or contact the maintainers directly.
